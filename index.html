Game Instructions

Your character begins at the far left of the map and your mission is to get it to the Goal Zone which is a picture that will change depending on the country you currently are.

Each level have different elements that will make your journey not so easy, as you progress in the game you'll come across:

  1. Columns: This will be present in all levels, and will stop you from walking in a straight direction, you need to get around them.
  2. Corona-Fog: Be careful, if the fog reaches your character, it will begin to lose HP until you eventually die, be aware that as the game progresses the fog will become increasingly faster.
  3. Virus: Don't touch them! these blos are very dangerous, they will take a full live out of your count.
  4. Lights On/Off: No, we don't mean a club. Since you are traveling during the night, as to avoid getting caught by the police. once the screen becomes clear (lights on) your player won't be able to move, so... be fast

Pro Tip: If you want 10 free lives at the beginning of the game, press the "Free Lives" button
